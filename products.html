INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTS

BITUMEN:

Bitumen is a viscous, black, and sticky form of petroleum. It is commonly used in the construction industry for various applications such as road surfacing, waterproofing, and roofing. Bitumen provides excellent durability, weather resistance, and adhesion properties, making it an ideal choice for infrastructure projects.

PETROCHEMICALS:

Petrochemicals are chemical compounds derived from petroleum or natural gas. They serve as the building blocks for a wide range of products, including plastics, fertilizers, solvents, fibers, and dyes. Petrochemicals play a crucial role in various industries, driving innovation and enabling the production of essential materials that enhance our daily lives.

SKO (Super Kerosene Oil):

SKO, also known as Super Kerosene Oil, is a versatile and widely used fuel. It is primarily used for domestic purposes such as lighting, heating, and cooking. SKO is a clean-burning fuel that provides a reliable and efficient energy source for households, especially in areas where access to electricity is limited.

LDO (Light Diesel Oil):

LDO, or Light Diesel Oil, is a type of diesel fuel with lower density and viscosity compared to conventional diesel. It is commonly used in industrial applications, such as in boilers, furnaces, and certain types of engines. LDO provides a cost-effective fuel option for various heating and power generation needs.

HSFO (High Sulfur Fuel Oil):

HSFO, or High Sulfur Fuel Oil, is a type of residual fuel oil with a higher sulfur content. It is typically used in marine vessels and large industrial boilers. HSFO has a high energy density, making it suitable for applications that require a significant amount of heat or power.
